FAQ's:

How does Tom Wahl's Rewards Program Work?  
Once you become a member, you will earn 2 points per $1 spent at any Tom Wahl's location. Earn points automatically when you order in our app and online at tomwahls.com. You must check in to earn points and redeem rewards, open your Tom Wahl's app & tap the “CHECK-IN” icon, and scan your QR code at the register, or provide the phone number associated with your account at checkout.
How do I Sign Up for Rewards?  
We recommend downloading Tom Wahl's Mobile app for the best experience. Find it in the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). Alternatively, guests can enroll online using this link: click here.
How do I Earn a Free Cheeseburger?  
New members who spend their first $10+ at any Tom Wahl's location will automatically receive a Free Cheeseburger! After your visit, check your email for confirmation!
Does Tom Wahl’s Mobile App ask for Location Information?  
Yes, however, sharing your location information is not required. Location information is used to locate restaurants nearest to you and help make your search quick and easy.
Do I need a smartphone to participate?  
Our mobile app is the best way to participate. To earn points in the restaurant without a mobile device, you will need to provide the phone number associated with the account. Non-App users can manage their account online using this link: click here.
What can I redeem points for?  
Points can be redeemed for most of your favorite menu items like Milkshakes, Cheeseburgers, Junker Plates, and more. To see the full list of current rewards, click here. Or visit the Reward Yourself section in the app or online to redeem your points.
I Forgot to Check-In. Can I get Points for a Previous Order?  
Absolutely! App users should submit a points request using the "Points Request" link found in the left side menu. Click the menu icon located in the upper left corner of the Mobile App welcome page to view. Alternatively, you can email us directly at rewards@tomwahls.com with a picture of your receipt along with all requested information. Please allow 48 hours for points to be added to your account.
How Do I Know How Many Points or Rewards I Have Earned?  
Your account balances are located on the welcome screen once you are logged into your account on the Mobile App. In the Guest Account Portal, you can find this in the Summary of Accounts section after logging in.
How do I Redeem a Reward?  
In-store during checkout, provide your QR/Check-in code from the Mobile App, OR provide the phone number linked to your rewards account. While ordering online or with the Mobile App, you will be shown a summary of applicable rewards when you review your order. Choose “Apply Reward” to apply eligible rewards. Further, while signed in to your account, open the settings menu and click on "Rewards".
I have Multiple Rewards. Can I Use More than One on a Single Order?  
Absolutely! However, coupons cannot be used in addition to redeemed rewards.
Will My Points Expire?  
Nope, your points will not expire! Once you redeem your points for a reward, you have 30 days to use that reward. In limited cases, certain promotions may place an expiration date on points awarded via the promotion.
Do my Rewards Expire?  
Rewards purchased with points typically expire after 30 days. Promotional offers expire in different time frames depending on the promotion. A summary of current rewards is found in the Mobile App (See "My Rewards") and the Guest Account Portal (See "Summary of Accounts").
How Long Does it Take for Points to Appear in My Account?  
Please allow up to 24 hours for points to be added to your account once you have checked out in the restaurant, on the app, or online. Generally speaking, you'll see them in your account much quicker!
How Long Do I Have to Use My Rewards after Redeeming Points?  
Once you redeem your points for a reward, you will have 30 days to redeem them.
Will I Earn Points on Delivery Fees or Third-Party Platforms?  
No, you cannot earn points on delivery fees or for orders placed via third-party delivery platforms, such as DoorDash, Grubhub, Uber Eats/Postmates.
Can I Earn Points on Gift Card Purchases?  
The purchase of a gift card will not earn you points. However, spending a pre-loaded gift card will earn you points.
Can I use the Mobile App to Pay?  
You can add a credit or debit card to your rewards account, and you can use this payment method for orders that you place, pick, and deliver in the mobile app or online. If you do, this will appear in the checkout flow.
I have a Gift Card. Can I save it to my Rewards Account to use as Payment?  
Gift cards cannot be stored as a payment method linked to your Rewards account at this time. However, you can pay with your gift card at checkout when ordering in-store, online, or via the Mobile App.
Besides Points, are there Other Ways to get Deals and Discounts?  
Our best deals are reserved for our Rewards members! From time to time, we will provide exclusive offers and promotions. You will receive notifications based on your communications preferences regarding promotional opportunities. If you’re not receiving any notifications, you may need to update your communication preferences in the Mobile App or Guest Account Portal. Contact us a rewards@tomwahls.com if you need assistance.
My Question Hasn’t Been Answered. What Should I Do?  
Click on the menu icon in the upper left corner of the Mobile App welcome page. Select the “Contact Us” link, and you will be taken to a feedback page, where you can ask a question or submit an inquiry, etc. You can also email us directly at rewards@tomwahls.com.
