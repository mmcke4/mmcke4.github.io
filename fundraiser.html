Host a Fundraiser

Need new uniforms? Extra money for a school trip? Tom Wahl's wants to help you reach your goals!

15%

of Sales Donated to Your Organization

The more people you invite to your fundraiser, the better! Enjoy great food with friends & family while raising money for a good cause.

How It Works

It's easy to set up — here's the process:

1

Request an Event

Fill out the form below to request an event at your local Tom Wahl's.

2

Choose a Night

Pick a Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday evening from 4:30–8:30 PM.

3

We Confirm

We'll review the details and confirm your event.

4

Promote Your Event

Spread the word using a flyer and/or social media. Note: flyers may not be distributed on or around the restaurant during the event.

5

Get Your Donation

15% of the net sales from your group will be donated to your organization!
